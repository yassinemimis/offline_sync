Hello! I'm Yassine (known as @yassinemimis on GitHub), a Junior Full Stack & Mobile Developer specializing in building production-ready cross-platform architectures.

I started Offline Sync Kit out of a real-world frustration: every time a team wants to build a Flutter app that works without an internet connection, they end up spending months reinventing the wheel (handling queues, database persistence, exponential retries, and manual conflict resolution).

My goal is to make enterprise-grade offline synchronization as simple and modular as plugging in Dio or Riverpod.
